# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

Konduit Ltd ("Konduit", "we", "our") operates konduit.tech and related services. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and your rights regarding that data. We align our practices with UK GDPR and South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

## What we collect

**Quote and contact form submissions.** When you submit a form on our site, we collect the information you provide: name, email address, company name, phone number, country of delivery, product requirements, and your message. We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry and manage an ongoing business relationship.

**Partner applications.** When you apply to join our partner network, we collect company details, country, services offered, team size, certifications, and contact information.

**Analytics data.** We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-focused analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not collect personal data. Plausible collects aggregate, anonymous usage data including page views, referral sources, browser type, and country. No individual visitors are identified or tracked.

**Technical data.** Our hosting provider (Vercel) processes standard server logs including IP addresses, request timestamps, and user agent strings. These are retained for operational and security purposes.

## What we do not collect

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising. We do not use Google Analytics. We do not run retargeting pixels. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

## How we use your data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes only:

- responding to quote requests and contact enquiries
- processing partner applications
- understanding aggregate site usage patterns to improve our content
- maintaining the security and performance of our website

## Third-party processors

We use the following services to process data on our behalf:

- **Vercel** (hosting and deployment)
- **HubSpot** (CRM and form handling)
- **Resend** (transactional email delivery)
- **Plausible Analytics** (website analytics, hosted in the EU)
- **Sanity** (content management, no personal data stored)

## Data retention

Quote requests and CRM records are retained for the duration of any active business relationship, plus 24 months after the last interaction. Analytics data is retained in aggregate form and cannot be linked to individuals.

## Your rights

Under UK GDPR and POPIA, you have the right to:

- access the personal data we hold about you
- request correction of inaccurate data
- request deletion of your data where there is no legitimate reason to retain it
- object to processing or request restriction
- lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (UK) or the Information Regulator (South Africa)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@konduit.tech.

## International transfers

Konduit operates from the United Kingdom. Data may be processed in the UK, EU, and United States through our service providers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international transfers.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
