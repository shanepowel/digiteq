# Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

This policy explains how Konduit uses cookies and similar technologies on konduit.tech.

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work and to provide information to site owners.

## How we use cookies

Konduit uses minimal cookies:

**Essential cookies.** We store a local preference when you accept our cookie notice. This is stored in your browser's local storage, not a tracking cookie.

**Analytics.** We use Plausible Analytics, which does not use cookies and does not track individual visitors. Plausible collects only aggregate, anonymous usage data.

We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or third-party tracking cookies.

## Managing cookies

Because we do not use tracking cookies, there is nothing to opt out of beyond clearing your browser's local storage. You can configure your browser to block all cookies, though this may affect site functionality.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top indicates when it was last revised.
